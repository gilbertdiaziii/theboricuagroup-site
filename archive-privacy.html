Privacy Policy – Archive

Last updated: December 29, 2025

Archive (“the App”) is operated by The Boricua Group LLC. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information we collect and how it is used.

Information We Collect

Account Information:
When you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication credentials to manage access to the app.

User Content:
Archive stores journal entries you create and optional photos you choose to attach to entries. This content is private and accessible only to you.

How We Use Information

Information is used to provide core app functionality, securely store and sync your content, manage accounts, and enable optional features.

Data Storage & Services

Archive uses trusted third-party services such as Supabase for authentication and data storage, RevenueCat for subscriptions, and Apple services for Apple Sign-In.

Photos & Permissions

Photo access is requested only if you choose to add a photo to an entry. Archive does not access your photo library without your permission.

Your Choices

You may edit or delete entries at any time and request account or data deletion by contacting us.
